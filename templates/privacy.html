🔒 Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2025
✅ Short version: We collect only what's needed to run T-PAL. We don't sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for anything beyond operating this service.
1 What Data We Collect

When you sign up and use T-PAL, we collect:

Data Why We Collect It Where Stored
Name To personalize your experience Our server (users.json / database)
Email address Account login & communication Our server (users.json / database)

2 What We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect or store:
  • Passwords (we use session-based login, no passwords stored)
  • Device information, IP addresses, or browser fingerprints
  • The actual question papers you generate
  • Payment card details (all payments are via UPI/QR, not stored by us)
  • Cookies beyond your active session
3 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only for:

  • Logging you into your account and maintaining your session
  • Enforcing free/premium usage limits (daily/hourly paper counts)
  • Contacting you about your premium upgrade status
  • Improving T-PAL (we may review which subjects/chapters are most used — anonymously)
4 Third-Party Services

T-PAL uses the following third-party services:

  • Render.com — hosts our web application. See Render's Privacy Policy.
  • WhatsApp — used only for premium upgrade communication (optional).

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising trackers.

5 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes. Period.

The only case we'd share data is if required by Indian law or a valid legal order.

6 Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data:

  • Our platform is served over HTTPS
  • User data is stored on Render.com's secured servers
  • No third-party has access to your account information

However, no internet service is 100% secure. Please use a unique email and do not share your account with others.
